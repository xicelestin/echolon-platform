## Customization Guide

### Research Before Sending
1. Check their website for recent news/updates
2. Identify specific pain points they might have
3. Note their industry/business model
4. Find mutual connections if possible

### Personalization Variables
- `[First Name]` - Always use first name
- `[Company Name]` - Full company name
- `[Specific observation]` - Something unique about their business
- `[Industry]` - Their specific industry
- `[Pain point]` - Relevant challenge they face

### Best Practices
✓ Keep emails under 150 words
✓ Lead with value, not features
✓ Include specific call-to-action
✓ Make it easy to say yes (calendar link, simple reply)
✓ Highlight 4-week free trial prominently
✓ Follow up 3-4 times before moving on
✓ Test different subject lines

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Day 0: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Follow-up #1 (social proof)
- Day 7: Follow-up #2 (industry-specific)
- Day 14: Follow-up #3 (breakup email)

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (aim for 40%+)
- Reply rate (aim for 5-10%)
- Meeting booked rate (aim for 2-5%)
- Trial signup rate
